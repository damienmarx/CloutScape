Welcome to the official CloutScape game guide! This guide will walk you through everything you need to know to start your journey in our immersive world.

### **Step 1: Create Your Account**

Creating an account is simple and secure, thanks to our Discord integration.

1.  Navigate to the `#bot-commands` channel.
2.  Type the command: `!register YourDesiredUsername`
3.  Check your Direct Messages (DMs) from the CloutScape bot for your unique password.

### **Step 2: Download the Game Client**

Our custom game client is lightweight and easy to use.

1.  In the `#bot-commands` channel, type: `!download`
2.  You'll receive a direct link to download the `client.jar` file.
3.  **Prerequisite**: Ensure you have Java 11 or higher installed on your computer. If not, you can download it from the official [Java website](https://www.java.com/en/download/).

### **Step 3: Log In and Play!**

1.  Double-click the `client.jar` file to launch the game.
2.  Enter the username and password you received in your DMs.
3.  Click "Login" and immerse yourself in the world of CloutScape!

### **Key Features & What to Do First**

-   **Explore the World**: Discover familiar landscapes and hidden secrets.
-   **Train Your Skills**: Level up your combat and non-combat skills to become a formidable player.
-   **Engage in PvP**: Head to the Wilderness and challenge other players to duels.
-   **Test Your Luck**: Visit the gambling area and try your hand at our various games of chance.
-   **Join Events**: Keep an eye on the `#events` channel for exciting server-wide activities.

We're excited to see you in the game! If you have any questions, don't hesitate to ask in the `#support` channel.

**Happy Scaping!**
